Study Abroad Counselling

Costs vary by country and program. USA: ₹25-60 lakhs/year, UK: ₹20-40 lakhs/year, Canada: ₹15-35 lakhs/year, Australia: ₹20-45 lakhs/year. We help identify scholarships, part-time work opportunities, and cost-effective universities to match your budget while maintaining quality education.

Yes! We provide comprehensive scholarship guidance including merit-based, need-based, and country-specific scholarships. Our team assists with application essays, documentation, and interview preparation to maximize your chances of securing financial aid worth ₹5-30 lakhs.

Essential documents include: academic transcripts, standardized test scores (GRE/GMAT, IELTS/TOEFL), Statement of Purpose (SOP), Letters of Recommendation (LORs), resume/CV, passport copy, financial documents, and portfolio (for specific programs). We guide you through the complete documentation process.

Our visa support includes DS-160/visa application form filling, financial document preparation, mock visa interviews, appointment scheduling guidance, and post-visa approval support. We have extensive experience with F-1 (USA), Tier 4 (UK), Study Permit (Canada), and Subclass 500 (Australia) visas.
